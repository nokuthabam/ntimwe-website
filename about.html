About

Introduction

  • Ownership

    Ntimwe Engineering Services Pty Ltd, is a 100% Black owned company, Level 1 BBBEE EME company which was formed in 2012 and started operating actively from January 2013. The company is proudly African and is motivated by the need to alleviate the African population from poverty through employment creation whilst providing market leading high quality service to its Clients.

  • Expertise

    Ntimwe Engineering Services Pty Ltd is led by a very experienced mechanical engineering professional with a wide range of skills in the built environment. The company managing director has previously worked at a senior level in the engineering consultancy industry in the past eighteen years. With hands-on experience and strategic management experience spanning almost twenty years, our client’s needs can be met with excellence. The company prides itself for employing only the best technical resources from the built environment industry to provide the best possible service for our clients. Our staff are our most important assets. We recognise that our clients procure our services because they have confidence in us. We know that the only way to meet their needs is by delivering good quality products and services.

  • Professional Affiliation

    The Principal Engineers of the company are professionally registered with the Engineering Council of South Africa (ECSA) and have membership with the South African Institution of Mechanical Engineers (SAIMechE), the American Society of Heating Refrigeration and Air-conditioning Engineers (ASHRAE) as well as the Green Building Council of South Africa (GBCSA).

  • Professional Association

    The company has developed professional associations with a number of progressive professional engineering companies in the built environment with different disciplines and complimentary experiences to form relationship that allow Ntimwe Engineering Services to provide a comprehensive professional services. Our range of services include all types of mechanical and electrical engineering related projects namely mechanical engineering design, electrical engineering as well as project management and cost management.

  • Company Vision

    The company strives to establish itself as a leading professional engineering consulting service provider emphasizing the utilization of appropriate and sustainable design solutions.

  • Company Mission

    The company’s mission is to provide sustainable and appropriate technical solutions with a positive social and environmental impact, thus ensuring value for all stakeholders.

  • Company Culture

    The company is an equal opportunity employer, committed to the uplifting of young Africans into the technical spheres and to inculcate a self-belief that will see our professionals rising to the highest level of the industry. The long term objective of the company is to grow into a well-managed, resourceful consulting engineering entity, offering all services within the built environment. However, it is the intention of the Principals to hand-pick only the best available resources from our built environment industry. This approach is motivated by our objective to provide excellent service to our clients. Without our clients we will soon be out of business. Thus our objective is to thoroughly satisfy our clients’ requirements in terms of meeting the constraints of quality, time and in budget. We thus endeavour to constantly and consistently communicate with our clients to ensure that their requirements are being met. We do not believe in “one solution fits all”. We tailor-make our services to meet our clients’ specific and unique needs.
